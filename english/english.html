Lesson 1

SIMPLE NEGATIVE AND QUESTION FORM

Hint
En inglés formamos la forma negativa de un verbo Ser o Estar
Posicionando la palabra NO directamente después del verbo.

    1. In English, we form the negative of the verb to be

    2. by placing not directly after the verb.

    • He is a teacher.

      He is not a teacher.

    • This is a book.

      This is not a book.

    We form questions with the verb to be by placing the subject after the verb.

    • He is a teacher.

      Is he a teacher?

    • This is a book.

      Is this a book?
